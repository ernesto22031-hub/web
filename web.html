About UrbanFit

More Than a Gym — A Community

UrbanFit was born from a simple idea: create a space where everyone feels welcome to pursue their fitness goals. We are a small, independent gym that focuses on quality over quantity — offering personalized attention, a supportive atmosphere, and the tools you need to succeed.

Our team of 4 dedicated professionals works every day to make UrbanFit a place where you can grow stronger, both physically and mentally. We believe fitness is for everyone, regardless of age or experience level.

Meet Our Founders

Driven by Passion

UrbanFit was founded in 2018 by four fitness enthusiasts who shared a common vision: to transform the fitness industry by creating an inclusive, modern gym that prioritizes member experience above everything else. With combined experience of over 40 years in personal training, nutrition coaching, and gym management, our founders built UrbanFit from the ground up with one goal in mind — making fitness accessible to everyone.

Today, we're proud of what we've built. From humble beginnings in a small space with basic equipment, UrbanFit has grown into a thriving community hub with state-of-the-art facilities, diverse class offerings, and a team of certified professionals dedicated to your success. But our journey doesn't stop here — we continue to innovate and expand, always keeping our members' needs at the heart of every decision.
